The Collserola hills are perfect when you want fresh air without a long journey. Choose a short, well-marked trail with gentle paths that little legs can manage.

Bring a simple picnic, look for sticks, leaves, and birds along the way, and keep the route flexible so you can turn back whenever the kids have had enough.
